Gmail

If you do not see an email from Greg L. Turnquist, check your Spam folder.

If found there, open it and click Not Spam. To ensure this doesn’t happen again:

  1. Open the email.
  2. Click the 3-dot menu on the top right.
  3. Select Filter messages like this.
  4. Click Create filter.
  5. Check the options:
    • Never send it to Spam
    • Always mark it as important
    • Also apply filter to matching conversations
    • Categorize as: Primary
  6. Click Create filter.

Gmail Tabs

If using Gmail tabs like Promotions:

  1. Open the Promotions tab.
  2. Drag the email from Greg L. Turnquist to the Primary tab.
  3. Click Yes when Gmail asks to do this for future messages.

Gmail App

On your phone, open the Gmail app. If the email is in spam:

  1. Open the email.
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu.
  3. Select Change labels.
  4. Check Inbox.
  5. Tap OK to confirm.

If using Promotions tab:

  1. Find the email and tap the 3-dot menu.
  2. Select Move to → Primary.

Yahoo! Mail

  1. Open the Spam folder and locate the email.
  2. Click Not Spam.
  3. To always allow:
    • Go to Settings → Filters
    • Create a filter where From contains greg@greglturnquist.com
    • Set to deliver to Inbox.

iPhone Mail App

  1. Go to the Junk folder.
  2. Find the email and swipe left.
  3. Tap More → Mark → Mark as Not Junk.

Outlook Mobile App

  1. Open the Outlook app.
  2. Open the email.
  3. Tap the 3-dot menu → Move to Focused Inbox.
  4. Choose Move this and future messages.

Outlook (Desktop)

  1. Right-click the email.
  2. Go to Junk → Never block sender.
  3. Click OK on the confirmation dialog.
  4. To add to contacts: Right-click sender → Add to Contacts.

Outlook.com

  1. If the email is in Junk, open it and click Wait, it's safe.
  2. Go to Settings → View all Outlook settings.
  3. Under Junk email, add greg@greglturnquist.com to Safe Senders.

AOL Mail

  1. Mark email from Greg L. Turnquist as Not Spam.
  2. Add to contacts by clicking the sender and selecting Add Contact.
  3. Enable images by clicking Don't block this sender.

Comcast

  1. Log into your Xfinity account and open Comcast webmail.
  2. If you find the email in Spam, open it.
  3. Click the Not Spam icon in the toolbar.
  4. Add to address book by clicking Add to Address Book in the sender's panel.
  5. Click Save.

EarthLink

  1. Check your Suspect Email folder.
  2. If found, move the email to Inbox and choose Add Contact.
  3. Open the email and use the Address Book Editor to save greg@greglturnquist.com.

AT&T

AT&T email is managed by Yahoo. Please follow the Yahoo instructions.

Mozilla Thunderbird

  1. If found in Junk, mark as Not Junk.
  2. Click Address Book.
  3. Select Personal Address Book → New Contact.
  4. Enter greg@greglturnquist.com and click OK.

Norton AntiSpam

  1. Start Norton and open Settings.
  2. Go to AntiSpam → Allowed List → Configure.
  3. Add greg@greglturnquist.com to the list and click OK.

McAfee

  1. Open McAfee and go to Web & Email Protection → Anti-Spam.
  2. Select Friends List.
  3. Add greg@greglturnquist.com to the list.

Trend Micro

  2. Select the email and click Approve Sender.
  3. Or lower the spam filter strength in Settings.

Cloudmark

  1. Open Cloudmark Options from the toolbar.
  2. Go to Whitelist tab and click Add.
  3. Add greg@greglturnquist.com and click OK.

SaneBox

  1. Open your email client and go to the @SaneLater folder.
  2. Drag email from Greg L. Turnquist to your Inbox.

Barracuda Networks

  1. In daily quarantine summary, click Whitelist next to the email.
  2. Or log in to Barracuda UI and add to Allowed Senders.

SpamAssassin

  2. Add this line: 
    whitelist_from greg@greglturnquist.com
